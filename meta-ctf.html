MetaCTF CyberGames 2020

Python, JavaScript, Bash, Linux

Loyola University Chicago's CTF team SeaShanty2 placed #254 out of 1,587 teams for the MetaCTF CyberGames 2020.

Our team SeaShanty2 worked on problems involving cryptography, open source intelligence (OSI), web exploitation, binary exploitation, reverse engineering, wireless technology, and forensics.

The languages we utilized were Python and JavaScript. In addition, we utilized several deciphering tools for the cryptography component and bash scripting/cookie forgery techniques for web exploitation.
